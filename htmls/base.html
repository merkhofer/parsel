Parsel

Parsel is a service to test your arbitrary combination of NLTK preprocessing techniques on a sample string. It'll PARSE your input, plus it speaks to snakes (Python/Black Market Snakes).

Parsel might let you get a sneak peek at your preprocessing techniques before you implement them, for a sanity check. To that end, check back soon for moar NLTK.

But the big idea for me, its creator, is to get up to speed with Flask, Jinja2, and nginx.
